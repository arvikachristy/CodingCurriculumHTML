Welcome to Engduino World!

The Engduino is a microcomputer based off of the popular Arduino platform - armed with a vicious array of sensors, LEDs and other interfaces, the Engduino makes the perfect companion for the budding programmer.

Using TouchDevelop you can program the Engduino to do a variety of useful things, and we challenge you to come up with your own innovative uses in addition to the ones suggested here
